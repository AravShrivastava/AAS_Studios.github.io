I felt a little relieved before so I smiled and that too just to console you."

Aoyama was stunned after learning some of Takeshi's thoughts on life.

Next day, during lunch Kayaba suggested strategy," We will sneak into the palace and slowly take our control over then confront Darwin. Once we have defeated Darwin, the kingdom will come under my control and the misery caused will come to an end. But that's not so easy."

ONE HOUR LATER...
