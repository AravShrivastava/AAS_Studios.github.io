TEACHERS 

One who prepare us for our future bright,
They give us true wisdom and light.


In our life from birth to death,
Teachers help in every breathe.


Now I'll tell you a story of a kid,
Who was born in day a mid.


According to priest, the time was not good,
But now what his parents could.


Anytime they start to repent,
Which in child's mind made a dent.


It made the child dull until that day,
When he met a teacher who made him active by just a play.


His teacher helped him a lot,
He became IAS by the things which his teacher taught.


All wisdom we get from the teacher,
Parents and teachers make us the best creature.


Without teachers we cannot fly high,
and to them we should never lie.
