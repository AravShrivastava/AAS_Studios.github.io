SWEET CHILDHOOD MEMORIES 

The memories of childhood from the past,
The play of whole day in the ground,
These memories are beautiful and will last,
In this life round.


First friend I made,
Friend as brave as a tiger,
All the trust I laid,
On him like a fighter.


Played, fell and got hurt a lot,
And at home mother took care,
This life was like this I thought,
And there was nothing to be aware.


Whatever we did and got in childhood,
Plays, gifts and surprizes,
Never after did it for our good,
And went on looking at the sun rises.


These memories are so beautiful,
Beautiful like a butterfly,
I also need a little to be careful,
But what will happen if I die.

By ~ Arav Shrivastava
