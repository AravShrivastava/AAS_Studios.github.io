VOICE OF CONSCIOUSNESS 

What is this inner voice in me?
Which decides whatever I see,
Which makes the spirit from the body free,
Free from the rules and others' vision,
Makes choices for life in every season.


What does this inner voice say?
Sometimes to sit when there is hay,
It is always right and shows the way,
Way for our future bright,
Makes choices which are always right.


This is the voice of consciousness,
Where heart speaks more than brain,
Following it will make you happy,
Even if all the evil spirits rain.

By Arav Shrivastava
